#definition
### Types
- `M` : [[Topological Space]]
- $\{U_{\alpha}\}_{\alpha\in\text{A}}$ : [[Covering]] of `M`
### Definition
$\{U_{\alpha}\}_{\alpha\in\text{A}}$  is a *locally finite covering* if for all `p` in `M`, `p` has a [[Neighborhood]] that intersects only finitely many of the sets $U_{\alpha}$ .
